About Me

I am a designer and street photographer. Design is my way of channeling ideas, while street photography is a hobby where I capture unexpected and captivating moments. I often explore different visual styles, using unique angles and creating heart-touching photographs.

Visual exploration has always been a part of who I am, whether through design or through the lens of my camera. Design allows me to bring ideas to life, while photography leads me to spontaneous moments that tell authentic stories. I enjoy pushing visual boundaries and creating works that build emotional connections with others.
